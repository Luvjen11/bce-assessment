Terms and Conditions

Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to Bristol Community Events (BCE). These Terms and Conditions outline the rules for using our website, booking events, and managing user accounts. By accessing or using our site, you agree to comply with these terms.

1. General Terms

BCE is a student-built platform designed to showcase how event management systems function. Users can browse, filter, and book events such as workshops, exhibitions, sports, and community gatherings. Admins can add, update, and manage events and venues.

  • Users must create an account to book tickets or view booking history.
  • All data provided by users should be accurate and up to date.
  • Admins reserve the right to edit or remove any event if necessary.

2. Event Bookings and Cancellations

  • Bookings are subject to venue capacity and booking deadline.
  • Users may update or cancel eligible bookings from their profile area.
  • Cancellation fees are calculated by event start date: 40+ days: no fee, 25-39 days: 40% fee, under 25 days: 100% fee.
  • Refund values shown in BCE are system-calculated records for coursework simulation.
  • Past events cannot be newly booked through the platform.

3. Discounts

  • Student discount: 10% (user declaration).
  • Advance booking discount: tiered discount based on days before event.
  • Discount values are applied during booking and shown in booking records/receipt view.

4. Multi-day Events

For events spanning multiple dates, users may select event day(s) presented by the system. Pricing is calculated using selected day count and booking quantity.

5. Waiting List Policy

When an event is full, users may join the waiting list. If a booking is cancelled, the earliest waiting entry may be marked as offered. Automated email notification is not guaranteed in this coursework implementation.

8. Payment and Technical Scope

  • Checkout is demonstrative for coursework and not a live commercial payment service.
  • Security controls include password hashing, parameterized SQL, and access control checks.

9. Legal, Ethical, and Privacy Notice

  • BCE respects user privacy and follows ethical web practices. Any personal data collected (such as names or emails) is used only for login and booking purposes.
  • We comply with the UK Data Protection principles for educational web projects.
  • Users should avoid sharing account details or passwords with others.

These Terms and Conditions are written to align with the assessment specification for the Web Development and Databases module. The policies are educational examples and not legally binding.
