Community Art Exhibition

General Information

Explore an inspiring collection of artwork from local schools and community artists, celebrating Bristol’s creative spirit. This exhibition showcases paintings, sculptures, photography, and digital art that reflect the diversity and imagination of the city.

Date:

April 5–10, 2025

Venue:

UWE Exhibition Centre, Bristol

Special Conditions:

Open to the public. Families and school groups welcome. No booking required.

Description:

The Community Art Exhibition is free to attend and aims to connect artists with the wider community. Visitors can view art displays, meet the creators, and participate in small interactive workshops hosted by local artists.

Entry Information

Admission: Open to all visitors — no booking necessary.

Price: Free Entry Dates: April 5–10, 2025

Join us anytime during the exhibition week. Walk-ins are welcome.
