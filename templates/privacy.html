Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

At Bristol Community Events (BCE), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website and services. This website is built as part of an educational project, and no real commercial transactions occur.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide event booking and account management features.

  • Personal Information: such as your name, email address, and password when you register an account.
  • Booking Information: event details, booking date, and ticket type.
  • Non-Personal Information: browser type, device, and general site usage statistics for improving our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used only to support the core functionality of the BCE platform:

  • To manage your account and bookings.
  • To send booking confirmations and updates about events.
  • To respond to user inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To improve website usability and functionality.

3. Data Protection and Security

BCE follows basic data protection practices to keep your information safe. Passwords are stored securely using encryption and cannot be viewed by administrators. No financial or payment data is collected or stored.

  • We use secure server connections (HTTPS) where possible.
  • User input is validated to prevent SQL injection or malicious code.
  • Data access is limited to authorized administrators for educational purposes only.

4. Cookies

This website uses basic session cookies to remember your login state. These cookies do not track or collect personal data and are automatically deleted when you log out or close your browser.

5. Data Sharing

BCE does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only used within this platform for managing your event bookings and account activities.

6. User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data you have provided.
  • Request correction or deletion of your account data.
  • Withdraw consent for email communications.

To make a request, contact us via info@bcevents.com.

7. Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as your BCE account is active. You may delete your account at any time, which will remove all personal data from our database.

8. Educational Disclaimer

This project is developed for academic purposes as part of the Web Development and Databases module. No real transactions or payments occur. Any personal data entered is simulated for demonstration only.

9. Changes to This Policy

BCE may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect improvements or course requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact:

  • Email: info@bcevents.com
  • Phone: +44 117 000 111
  • Address: Bristol, UK

This Privacy Policy is part of an educational project and is not legally binding. It is created to demonstrate understanding of legal, ethical, and professional practices in web development.
