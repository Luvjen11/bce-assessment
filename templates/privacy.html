Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Bristol Community Events (BCE), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website and services. This website is built as part of an educational project, and no real commercial transactions occur.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide event booking and account management features.

  • Personal Information: such as your name, email address, and password when you register an account.
  • Booking Information: event details, booking date, and ticket type.
  • Non-Personal Information: browser type, device, and general site usage statistics for improving our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Process event bookings, updates, and cancellations.
  • Apply student and advance-booking discounts.
  • Manage waiting-list statuses (for example: waiting, offered).
  • Support admin reporting and platform maintenance.

3. Data Protection and Security

  • Passwords are stored as hashes and are not visible in plain text.
  • Database queries use parameterized statements to reduce SQL injection risk.
  • User data rendered in templates is protected by Jinja auto-escaping.

5. Payments

BCE uses a demonstration checkout flow for coursework purposes. No live payment gateway is integrated and no real card processing is performed by this website.

6. Data Sharing

BCE does not sell personal data. Information is used only within this platform for account and booking functionality.

7. User Rights

You may request access or correction of your stored data. Account/data deletion requests can be made via the project contact channel and handled by administrators where appropriate.

8. Waiting List Data

If an event is full, users may join a waiting list. On cancellation, the system may change the earliest waiting record to an offered status. This project does not guarantee real-time external notifications.

This Privacy Policy is part of an educational project and is not legally binding. It is created to demonstrate understanding of legal, ethical, and professional practices in web development.
