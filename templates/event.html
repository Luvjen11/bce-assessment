Bristol Jazz Festival 2025

General Information

The Bristol Jazz Festival 2025 celebrates the city’s vibrant music scene, featuring performances from talented local and international artists. Enjoy three days of live jazz, food stalls, and cultural showcases in one of Bristol’s most iconic venues.

Date:

March 12–14, 2025

Venue:

Arnolfini Arts Centre, Bristol

Special Conditions:

Formal dress preferred. Doors open at 5:00 PM each day.

Description:

This event includes multiple sessions throughout the weekend. Attendees can purchase single-day or weekend tickets. Live performances include jazz ensembles, instrumental workshops, and late-night jam sessions.

Ticket Information

Standard Ticket: Access for one day.

Price: £15 Sales end: March 10, 2025

Weekend Pass: Access to all sessions for all three days.
