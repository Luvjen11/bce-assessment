Creative Art Workshop

General Information

Join a weekend of painting, crafting, and creative exploration led by local artists. This hands-on workshop encourages self-expression and learning through art, suitable for both beginners and experienced creators.

Date:

March 22, 2025

Venue:

Creative Space B, Bristol

Special Conditions:

All materials provided. Please wear comfortable clothing that you don’t mind getting messy.

Description:

This full-day session includes guided painting and crafting sessions, lunch break, and a collaborative art showcase at the end. Participants will leave with their own artwork and a certificate of completion.

Ticket Information

Workshop Pass: Includes access to all sessions, materials, and refreshments.

Price: £30 Sales end: March 20, 2025
