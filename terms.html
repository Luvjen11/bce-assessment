Terms and Conditions

Last updated: November 2025

Welcome to Bristol Community Events (BCE). These Terms and Conditions outline the rules for using our website, booking events, and managing user accounts. By accessing or using our site, you agree to comply with these terms.

1. General Terms

BCE is a student-built platform designed to showcase how event management systems function. Users can browse, filter, and book events such as workshops, exhibitions, sports, and community gatherings. Admins can add, update, and manage events and venues.

  • Users must create an account to book tickets or view booking history.
  • All data provided by users should be accurate and up to date.
  • Admins reserve the right to edit or remove any event if necessary.

2. Event Bookings and Cancellations

Users can book available events through their BCE account. Each venue has a limited capacity, and bookings will close once capacity is reached or the last booking date passes.

  • Bookings can be updated or cancelled through the user’s account dashboard.
  • Cancellations are subject to the following refund and fee rules:
    • 40+ days before event: No cancellation fee (full refund).
    • 25–39 days before event: 40% of booking price will be charged.
    • Within 25 days of event: No refund available.
  • Refunds are processed within 7–14 working days (simulated for coursework purposes).
  • Users cannot transfer bookings to another person.

3. Discounts

The BCE system applies automatic discounts based on booking conditions:

  • Student Discount: 10% off ticket price (no ID required – user confirmation only).
  • Advance Booking Discount: Between 5%–20% depending on how early the booking is made (up to 60 days in advance).

Discounts are calculated automatically during the checkout process and shown on the generated booking receipt.

4. Free and Paid Events

  • Some community events are marked as “Free.” Users can still book them to reserve a spot.
  • Paid events display their price clearly, including any discount applied.

5. Dynamic Pricing Policy

Admins may reduce ticket prices for events where less than 50% of tickets are sold within 10 days of the start date. The reduction will be at least 25% to encourage more participation.

6. Waiting List Policy

If an event is fully booked, users may join a waiting list. If a cancellation occurs, the first person on the list will be contacted automatically and given priority to book.

7. Admin and Management Terms

  • Admins must log in using their secure admin credentials.
  • Admins can add, update, or remove events and venues.
  • Admins can view all bookings, generate reports, and manage user accounts.
  • Reports may include event profits, total bookings, and available tickets.

8. Technical and Security Information

  • BCE implements security measures such as password hashing, input validation, and SQL injection prevention.
  • User passwords are stored securely and cannot be retrieved once created.
  • No real payment systems are integrated — transactions are simulated for educational purposes.

9. Refunds Policy

Refunds are only applicable for cancellations that meet the timelines outlined above. All refund values will be displayed in the user's booking history and receipt. Refunds cannot be processed for missed events or late cancellations.

10. Legal, Ethical, and Privacy Notice

  • BCE respects user privacy and follows ethical web practices. Any personal data collected (such as names or emails) is used only for login and booking purposes.
  • We comply with the UK Data Protection principles for educational web projects.
  • Users should avoid sharing account details or passwords with others.

These Terms and Conditions are written to align with the assessment specification for the Web Development and Databases module. The policies are educational examples and not legally binding.
