Who We Are

Bristol Community Events (BCE) is a local initiative that brings together people, artists, and organizations to promote events, creativity, and shared experiences in Bristol. From workshops and exhibitions to music festivals and cultural gatherings, BCE connects the city through vibrant community engagement.
